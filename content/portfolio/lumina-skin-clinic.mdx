## The challenge

Lumina Skin Clinic had built a strong reputation in their neighbourhood through word of mouth — experienced dermatologists, consistent results, genuinely satisfied patients. But their digital presence told a completely different story.

The site was a generic WordPress template, last updated two years before we got involved. It had no online booking, a single wall-of-text services page, and photographs lifted from a stock library. On mobile — which accounts for the majority of local healthcare searches — it loaded slowly and was difficult to navigate. The clinic was not appearing in Google's local map pack for searches like "dermatologist near me" or "skin clinic Chennai", despite serving patients in that city for over a decade.

The founder's diagnosis was accurate: **patients were finding the clinic, judging the website, and quietly choosing a competitor whose digital presence felt more trustworthy.** Consultation bookings came almost entirely through phone calls and referrals. There was no way to book outside office hours, and the front desk was fielding the same qualifying questions on repeat.

Three things needed fixing: the first impression, the conversion path, and local search visibility.

## Our approach

We started with a discovery phase — reviewing the existing site, auditing local search performance, and interviewing the clinical team about which treatments drove the most revenue and required the most patient education before a consult. That shaped everything that followed.

**Platform and booking.** We built a custom clinic platform on a performant, mobile-first stack. At the centre of it is an integrated online consultation booking system — patients can select a treatment category, choose a preferred dermatologist, pick a time slot, and confirm in under two minutes. Automated reminders go out at 24 hours and two hours before the appointment.

**Treatment catalogue.** Each core treatment — from medical-grade peels and laser resurfacing to acne management and aesthetic injectables — has its own dedicated page. The pages are written for patients, not search engines: plain language about what to expect, how many sessions, downtime, and who is a good candidate. This depth of information does two things. It answers the questions patients were calling to ask, and it gives each page a genuine reason to rank.

**Before-and-after gallery.** Aesthetic patients want evidence. We built a consent-compliant before-and-after gallery, organised by treatment type. The consent and privacy flow was designed carefully — every image is displayed with explicit patient permission documented, in line with responsible clinical marketing practice.

**Local SEO.** We restructured the site architecture around Chennai-specific signals, built out a properly optimised Google Business Profile, established consistent NAP citations across directories, and created location-relevant content targeting the neighbourhoods the clinic actually serves. Schema markup for medical businesses and individual dermatologist profiles was implemented throughout.

> **Stat —** Within three months of launch, the clinic's Google Business Profile moved from outside the local map pack to a consistent top-three position for their primary treatment keywords in Chennai.

## The outcome

Results took shape steadily over the four months following launch — no overnight spike, just a clear and measurable upward trend that compounded as the new pages began to earn organic positions.

Online consultation bookings roughly tripled compared to the equivalent period the previous year. More meaningfully, the mix shifted: the front desk is no longer the primary booking channel. Patients now arrive at the clinic having already read about their intended treatment, which shortens consultations and improves conversion from first appointment to booked course.

No-show rates dropped noticeably after the automated reminder system went live — the clinic estimates it recovered several appointment slots per week that were previously going unfilled.

The treatment catalogue pages became the site's strongest organic traffic drivers. Several treatment-specific pages now rank on page one for relevant Chennai search terms, bringing in patients who had no prior awareness of Lumina Skin Clinic.

The clinic's Google Business Profile reviews increased in volume as well — partly because the post-visit follow-up sequence, which previously did not exist, now includes a gentle prompt to share feedback.
